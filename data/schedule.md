# 2026 FIFA World Cup Schedule

## Tournament Overview
- Dates: June 11 - July 19, 2026
- Host Countries: United States, Mexico, Canada
- Teams: 48 (expanded from 32)
- Total Matches: 104
- Venues: 16 stadiums across 16 host cities

## Key Dates
- Opening Match: June 11, 2026 - Mexico vs South Africa at Mexico City Stadium
- Group Stage: June 11 - June 27, 2026
- Round of 32: June 28 - July 3, 2026
- Round of 16: July 4 - July 7, 2026
- Quarter-finals: July 9 - July 11, 2026
- Semi-finals: July 14 - July 15, 2026 (Dallas and Atlanta)
- Third-place Match: July 18, 2026 at Hard Rock Stadium, Miami
- Final: July 19, 2026 at MetLife Stadium, New York/New Jersey

## Group Stage Format
- 12 groups of 4 teams each
- Top 2 from each group plus 8 best third-placed teams advance to Round of 32
- Three full days of rest between matches for 103 of 104 matches
